## Overview
Dark Eagle is America's answer to [Avangard](avangard.md) and [DF-17](df-17.md): a truck-launched two-stage booster carrying the Common Hypersonic Glide Body (C-HGB), shared with the US Navy's sea-launched Conventional Prompt Strike program. Unlike its Russian and Chinese counterparts, it is explicitly **conventional** — designed to hit time-critical, heavily defended, high-value targets (think air-defense nodes and command sites) within minutes at theater range.

## Why it matters
- **The US enters the HGV race** — first Army hypersonic battery, closing a publicized capability gap.
- **Conventional prompt strike:** speed used for tactical surprise rather than nuclear delivery.
- **Joint architecture:** one glide body shared across Army trucks and Navy submarines (Zumwalt, Virginia-class).
