# 🇺🇸 Armory: United States

**Doctrine snapshot:** a mature triad (silo ICBMs, submarines, bombers) prioritizing *reliability and precision* over novelty, the world's most combat-proven cruise missile fleet, and — after years of trailing in hypersonics — fielded boost-glide weapons plus the deepest missile-*defense* portfolio anywhere.

## Headliners in this knowledge base

| System | Class | Signature capability |
|---|---|---|
| [Minuteman III](../inventory/minuteman-iii.md) | ICBM | 50+ years on continuous alert |
| [Trident II D5](../inventory/trident-ii-d5.md) | SLBM | Most accurate sub-launched missile; arms the UK too |
| [Tomahawk Block V](../inventory/tomahawk.md) | Cruise | 2,300+ combat firings, now anti-ship capable |
| [LRHW Dark Eagle](../inventory/lrhw-dark-eagle.md) | Hypersonic glide | First US Army hypersonic battery |

## Also notable (not yet profiled)

- **LGM-35A Sentinel** — Minuteman III's replacement, 2030s
- **AGM-158 JASSM / LRASM** — stealthy air-launched cruise & anti-ship family
- **Conventional Prompt Strike** — Navy version of Dark Eagle's glide body (Zumwalt destroyers, Virginia subs)
- **SM-6** — one missile doing air defense, terminal BMD, and anti-ship duty
- **Defensive suite** — GMD, THAAD, Aegis/SM-3, Patriot PAC-3 (see [Air Defense](../classes/air-defense-interceptors.md))

## Reading the arsenal
The US bet: networks, accuracy, and stealth beat raw speed — a bet now hedged by crash hypersonic programs. Its unique asset is the defensive layer cake no rival matches.
